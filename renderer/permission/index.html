Screenchart can't see your screen

Your last capture came back black. macOS needs your permission before Screenchart can read what's on screen.

  1. 1 Open System Settings → Privacy & Security.
  2. 2 Select Screen & System Audio Recording.
  3. 3 Turn on the switch next to Screenchart.
  4. 4 Quit and reopen Screenchart.
